# ACTIVATE-REVEAL-CONVERT Framework (14-Day High-Converting Sales Sequence)

The most powerful sequence structure for converting leads who downloaded a lead magnet or signed up for a free resource. Built on psychological principles that create ownership, demonstrate value, and drive action.

```
ACTIVATE (0-3) ──→ REVEAL (3-7) ──→ CONVERT (8-14)
```

## The 10-Email Structure

| # | Day | Email Name | Purpose |
|---|-----|------------|---------|
| 1 | 0 | DELIVER + GIFT | Free sample + "try this in 2 min" |
| 2 | 1 | ACTIVATION PUSH ← MOST IMPORTANT | "Did you try it?" + comparison test |
| 3 | 3 | THE REVEAL | Why it worked (methodology, not magic) |
| 4 | 5 | THE SYSTEM | "Don't know where to start" → full picture |
| 5 | 7 | SOCIAL PROOF | What others are building |
| 6 | 8 | THE OFFER | Full pitch + options |
| 7 | 9 | THE MATH | ROI breakdown, "one win = paid for" |
| 8 | 10 | OBJECTION | "Can't I just ___?" → here's the difference |
| 9 | 11 | ANTI-VISION | "A year from now you'll either..." |
| 10 | 14 | WHY DIDN'T YOU? ← NON-BUYERS | "What held you back?" (converts + feedback) |

## Phase 1: ACTIVATE (Days 0-3)

Goal: Get them to USE what you gave them within 48 hours. If they use it, they own it psychologically.

**Email 1: DELIVER + GIFT (Day 0)** — Deliver resource + "try this in 2 min" quick win
**Email 2: ACTIVATION PUSH (Day 1)** ← MOST IMPORTANT — "Did you try it?" + comparison test
**Email 3: THE REVEAL (Day 3)** — WHY the quick win worked (methodology, not magic)

## Phase 2: REVEAL (Days 5-7)

Goal: Show them the bigger picture.

**Email 4: THE SYSTEM (Day 5)** — Complete framework; position offer as the vehicle
**Email 5: SOCIAL PROOF (Day 7)** — Real results from real customers

## Phase 3: CONVERT (Days 8-14)

Goal: Close with logic, objection handling, and urgency.

**Email 6: THE OFFER (Day 8)** — Full pitch + all options
**Email 7: THE MATH (Day 9)** — ROI breakdown: "one win = paid for"
**Email 8: OBJECTION (Day 10)** — "Can't I just ___?" → why shortcuts don't work
**Email 9: ANTI-VISION (Day 11)** — "A year from now you'll either..."
**Email 10: WHY DIDN'T YOU? (Day 14)** — For non-buyers: feedback + last conversion shot

## Key Psychological Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| 48-hour window | Day 0-1 emails are critical — activate before they forget |
| Endowment effect | Get them to TRY the quick win — once used, it becomes "theirs" |
| Show > tell | Comparison test beats marketing copy |
| Anti-vision | Make them feel the cost of inaction |
| Ask non-buyers | "Why didn't you?" often converts + provides feedback |

## Segmentation

- Track who opens/clicks Day 1 activation email
- Non-activators: Send extra nudge Day 2
- Activators: Fast-track through reveal phase
- Purchased → Exit to customer sequence
