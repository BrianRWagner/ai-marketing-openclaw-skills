# Lead Nurture Sequence (Pre-Sale, 8 Emails)

## Email 1: Deliver + Introduce (Immediate)
Deliver lead magnet. Brief intro. Preview what's coming.

## Email 2: Expand on Topic (Day 2-3)
Related insight. Establish expertise. Light CTA to content.

## Email 3: Problem Deep-Dive (Day 4-5)
Articulate their problem deeply. Hint at solution.

## Email 4: Solution Framework (Day 6-8)
Your approach/methodology. Educational, not salesy.

## Email 5: Case Study (Day 9-11)
Real results. Specific and relatable. Soft CTA.

## Email 6: Differentiation (Day 12-14)
Why your approach is different. Address alternatives.

## Email 7: Objection Handler (Day 15-18)
Common concern addressed. FAQ or myth-busting.

## Email 8: Direct Offer (Day 19-21)
Clear pitch. Strong value proposition. Urgency if available.
