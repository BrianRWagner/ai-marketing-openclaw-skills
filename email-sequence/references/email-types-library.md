# Email Types Library — Full Reference

Use as an audit checklist and implementation guide.

## Onboarding Emails

### New Users Series
Trigger: User signs up (free/trial) | Goal: Activate, drive to aha moment | Sequence: 5-7 emails/14 days

### New Customers Series  
Trigger: User converts to paid | Goal: Reinforce decision, drive adoption | Sequence: 3-5 emails/14 days
Note: Focus on reinforcement and expansion, not conversion.

### Key Onboarding Step Reminder
Trigger: User hasn't completed critical setup step | Goal: Nudge completion
Approach: Remind what they started. Explain why step matters. Make it easy. Offer help.

### New User Invite
Trigger: Existing user invites teammate | Recipient: The invited person
Sequence: Accept invite → Day 2 reminder → Day 5 final reminder

## Retention Emails

### Upgrade to Paid
Trigger: Trial ending or high engagement | Sequence: 3-5 emails
Structure: Value summary → Feature comparison → Social proof → Urgency → Last chance

### Upgrade to Higher Plan
Trigger: Approaching plan limits or using higher-tier features
Approach: Acknowledge their growth → Show what next tier unlocks → Quantify value

### Ask for Review
Trigger: Customer milestone (30/60/90 days, positive support interaction, renewal)
Best timing: After positive NPS, after result achieved. NOT after billing issues.
Approach: Short. Thank them. Direct link to review platform.

### Proactive Support Outreach
Trigger: Signs of struggle (usage drop, failed actions, repeated help doc views)
Approach: Genuine concern. Specific. Offer direct help. No sales pitch.

### Product Usage Report
Trigger: Weekly/monthly | Goal: Demonstrate value, drive engagement
Include: Key metrics, comparison to prior period, achievements, suggestions

### NPS Survey
Trigger: Quarterly or post-milestone | Format: Single email, simple question
Follow-up: Promoters → review ask | Passives → "what would make it a 10?" | Detractors → personal outreach

### Referral Program
Timing: After positive NPS, after customer achieves result, at renewal
Approach: Remind of success. Explain offer. Make sharing easy. Show benefit for both.

## Billing Emails

### Switch to Annual
Value prop: Calculate exact savings. Lock in current price. Easy one-click switch.
Best timing: Around monthly renewal. End of year. Price increase announcement.

### Failed Payment Recovery — 4-Email Sequence
Day 0: Friendly notice, update payment link
Day 3: Reminder, service may be interrupted
Day 7: Urgent, account will be suspended
Day 10-14: Final notice, what they'll lose
Approach: Assume it's an accident. Clear CTA to update payment.

### Cancellation Survey
Trigger: User cancels | Goal: Learn why, opportunity to save
Questions: Primary reason, what could we do better, would anything change your mind

### Upcoming Renewal Reminder
Trigger: 14-30 days before renewal | Required for annual subscriptions
Include: Renewal date/amount, how to update payment, any changes

## Win-Back Emails

### Expired Trials — 4-Email Sequence
Day 1: Trial ended, what you're missing
Day 7: What held you back? (feedback)
Day 14: Incentive offer (discount, extended trial)
Day 30: Final reach-out

Segment by engagement: High → remove conversion friction | Low → offer fresh start | None → ask what happened

### Cancelled Customers — 3-Email Sequence  
Day 30: What's new since you left
Day 60: We've addressed [common cancellation reason]
Day 90: Special offer to return
Approach: No guilt, no desperation. Genuine updates. Personalize based on cancellation reason.

## Campaign Emails

### Monthly Roundup / Newsletter
Content mix: Product updates, customer stories, educational content, company news
Best practices: Consistent day/time. Scannable. One primary CTA.

### Seasonal Promotions
Common: New Year, Black Friday, fiscal year-end, back to work
Structure: Announcement → Reminder → Last chance

### Product Updates
Include: What's new, why it matters, how to use it, who asked for it
Segment: Users who'd benefit most. Feature requesters. Power users first.

### Pricing Update
Timeline: Announce 30-60 days before → 14-day reminder → 7-day final notice
Approach: Transparent. Explain why. Grandfather if possible. Give options.
