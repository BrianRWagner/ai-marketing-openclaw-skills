### 3. Easy Keyword Ranking

### 7. SEO Audit

Template-driven pages at scale. Location pages, comparison pages, integration pages.

### 41. Content Repurposing
Transform one piece into multiple formats. Blog → Twitter thread → YouTube → podcast.

### 56. Proprietary Data Content

### 67. Internal Linking
Build topical clusters. Strategic internal linking distributes authority.

### 74. Knowledge Base SEO
